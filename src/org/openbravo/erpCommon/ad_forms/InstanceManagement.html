Activation

A System Key is required to activate an Openbravo Professional or Basic Edition.

You will receive a System Key when you purchase a subscription.

If you are connected to the Internet, you can activate the system online.

Otherwise, you need to provide a valid Activation File that can be downloaded from  Openbravo Butler.

If you have changed your subscription details, the Activation must be refreshed for them to be in place.

If you are connected to the Internet, you can do so online.

Otherwise, you need to provide a new Activation File that can be downloaded from  Openbravo Butler.

If you have changed your subscription details, the Activation must be refreshed for them to be in place.

If you are connected to the Internet, you can do so online.

Otherwise, you need to provide a new Activation File that can be downloaded from  Openbravo Butler.

To reactivate an expired Professional or Basic Edition, you need to refresh your Activation File.

If you are connected to the Internet, you can do so online.

Otherwise, you need to provide a new Activation File that can be downloaded from  Openbravo Butler.
 
 

You may revert back to the Community Edition from your Professional/Basic Edition by pressing the Deactivate button.

Please notice that all installed commercial modules must be uninstalled or disabled first.
 

Alternatively, you may cancel your subscription by pressing the Cancel Subscription button.

Your system will remain a Professional/Basic Edition and you will continue to be able to use your currently installed commercial modules but you will no longer able to install new commercial modules or update existing ones.
